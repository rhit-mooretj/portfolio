My passion for computers started at an early age. The earliest memory I have was setting up my Grandma's TV while my family helped her move in at the age of 4. Normally, entertaining a kid while move in isn't something people are focusing on, so I took matters into my own hands and found a way to make it work. Since then, I have committed myself to software.

Before showing up to Rose, I had not taken a computer science course. I found ways to learn about the interworkings of computers, and then learned how to actually solve problems with code. I have been able to build upon the early understanding of computers I gained in my youth and used that to influence how I solve problems. So far this has proved to be successful in my education, and it has helped prepare me for life outside of Rose.

After graduation I am committed to making a substantial impact on countless lives. I have committed myself to serving people throughout my life so far, and I want to continue that through code. My Artificial Intelligence minor will help me make impacts during the revolution of the new technology, and my Engineering Management masters degree will help me lead the teams along the way. I've found my way to make an impact on the world.
